Work

Projects

OLA Booking Analysis
• Analyzed a total booking value of ₹7M, finding that Prime Sedan and Prime SUV contributed over ₹1.58M each in revenue.
• Discovered a 17.91% driver cancellation rate, with peak drop-offs during high-demand hours, impacting customer trust.
• Found that Mini cabs had the lowest average rating (4.02), indicating service improvement opportunities.
• Identified cash as the dominant payment method, contributing the largest share of transactions.

Starbucks sentiment analysis
• Analyzed 700+ customer reviews to identify sentiment trends across multiple Starbucks locations.
• Discovered that 78% of reviews were negative, with only 17.3% positive and 4.68% neutral, highlighting significant customer dissatisfaction.
• Crafted interactive Power BI dashboards to visualize sentiment distribution, time-based review patterns, and location-specific insights.

Tata Consultancy Services (Virtual Simulation)
• Developed an interactive executive dashboard visualizing key business metrics, including revenue trends, revenue and quantity by country, and top 10 customers by revenue.
• Analyzed revenue trends for 2011, identifying a peak in November at ₹1.49M at 144.26% increase from the January revenue of ₹0.61M
• Visualized revenue and quantity by country, revealing the Netherlands as the highest revenue-generating region at ₹0.24M and also leading in units sold with 200k units. Germany followed in revenue at ₹0.22M.
• Identifying and presented the top 10 customers by revenue, with the leading customer contributing ₹0.25M.

Netflix Movie Analysis
• Identified Drama as the most frequent genre, appearing in 14.5% of the dataset, followed by Comedy and Action.
• Found Spider-Man: No Way Home as the most popular movie with a score of 5,083.95, while The United States vs. Billie Holiday had the lowest popularity at 13.35.
• Categorized movies into four rating tiers, with 25.5% classified as "popular" and 18.5% of popular movies belonging to the Drama genre.
• Discovered 2020 as the year with the highest number of movie releases, reflecting a peak in content production.
